About us

About this Café

Fuzzy Mug Café was born from the shared passion of three friends who studied Café Management together at university. We dreamed of creating a cozy and relaxing space that everyone could enjoy high-quality food and beverages at affordable prices.

Our Target

During our time as students, we often struggled to find a cozy place to relax between classes — there weren't any cafés nearby where we could unwind. That's why we decided to open a café close to the university, creating a welcoming space where students and anyone that need a break can rest, recharge, and enjoy a good time.

Our café awarded a Grade A certification for top hygiene and cleanliness standards.

Recipent of the prestigious Michelin 1-Star Award in 2024.

Awarded the Great Taste Award in 2023 for our delicious offerings.

Honored with the TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice Award in 2023.
